Season 1 — Episode 10: Clearing Filters

Season 1: Identifying Consciousness

Filters shape what we see.
Clearing them allows truth to flow upstream.

What Filters Are

Mental filters are biases, assumptions, and past experiences that distort perception of the present moment. Recognizing them is the first step to conscious clarity.

Impact of Filters

Filters influence thoughts, emotions, and reactions. They create misunderstandings, unnecessary stress, and limit awareness.

Practice — Filter Awareness

  • Choose a recent situation where you felt reactive.
  • Identify assumptions or judgments you applied.
  • Imagine perceiving the situation without those filters.
  • Notice how perspective and feelings shift.

Repeating this strengthens the ability to see clearly and respond consciously.

Upstream Application

Clearing filters fosters objectivity, presence, and conscious choice. It is foundational to aligning with your upstream self.
