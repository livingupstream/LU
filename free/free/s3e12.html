Season 3 — Episode 12: Preparing for Season Transition

Season 3: Closing Insights and Setting Up for Season 4

Completion of one phase is the doorway to the next.
Reflection and preparation ensure seamless upstream progression.

Reflection and Consolidation

Review practices, exercises, and lessons from Season 3. Observe growth areas, strengths, and recurring challenges. Reflection consolidates learning and primes the mind for deeper engagement in Season 4.

Setting Intentions

Define personal intentions for the next season. Consider what upstream practices you wish to deepen, which habits to maintain, and what new areas of awareness to explore.

Practice — Transition Preparation

  • Reflect on all Season 3 episodes and exercises completed.
  • Identify top 3 lessons that had the greatest impact on your awareness.
  • Set clear intentions for your practice in the upcoming season.
  • Journal thoughts, insights, and goals to carry forward.

Proper preparation ensures continuity and deeper alignment as you enter the next stage of Living Upstream.
